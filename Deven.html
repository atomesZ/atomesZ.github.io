Group creation

Our group was formed by affinity since we were all four in the same class. After much discussion, we noticed that we had a similar vision of the project and that we wanted to implement this project together. We decided to name our team REST'art, "REST" being the first letter of each of our first names, "art" representing the art of design and development of video games. Finally, the whole "REST'art" meaning "restart" in reference to this iconic button video games when the player wants to start a game. It also highlights the fact that our games will be playable several times without ever getting tired, on the contrary, restarting a game allows the player to improve and establish a strategy against his opponents and developing a competitive aspect.
